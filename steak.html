Steak Recipe

Ingredients

2 ribeye steaks (about 1 inch thick)
2 tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic (minced)
1 tbsp fresh thyme or rosemary (optional)
Salt and freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
1 tbsp butter (optional)

Procedure

  1. Take the steaks out of the fridge and let them come to room temperature for about 20-30 minutes.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Season both sides of the steaks with salt and pepper.
  4. Once the pan is hot, add the steaks and sear for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until desired doneness is achieved.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, add the minced garlic and herbs (optional), and spoon the infused oil over the steaks for extra flavor.
  6. Remove the steaks from the pan and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  7. Optional: Top the steaks with butter for a richer taste.
  8. Serve with your choice of sides and enjoy your perfectly cooked steak!
