Lumpia Recipe

Ingredients

1 pack lumpia wrapper
1 cup ground pork
1/2 cup carrots, julienned
1/2 cup green beans, chopped
1/2 onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
Oil for frying

Procedure

  1. In a pan, saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the ground pork and cook until browned.
  3. Add the carrots, green beans, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir and cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Let the filling cool, then wrap a spoonful of the mixture in lumpia wrappers.
  5. Heat oil in a frying pan and fry the lumpia until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Drain excess oil and serve with sweet chili sauce. Enjoy!
